## 2. Fix `DEAP_MUTATION_PROB` — it's effectively broken again (High impact)

You fixed the `< 1.0` bug but overcorrected to `0.0015`. With 9 genes, the probability that **any** gene mutates per individual is only 1.3%. In a population of 20, you get on average **0.27 mutations per generation** — meaning most generations have zero mutations at all. DEAP is running 8 generations of pure tournament selection with a frozen gene pool. It's not evolving.

The standard convention is `1/n_genes`:

